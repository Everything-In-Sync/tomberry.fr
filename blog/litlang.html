My new blog – Life in the Languedoc

  • October 2nd, 2019

Several of you have suggested I create a blog reporting my life here in France. So I’ve done it.

I love to write and have been writing for more than 40 years. My intention in retirement was to review all I’ve written over the years and maybe pull it together into a book. But it was never clear to me what this book should be: an autobiography? A memoir? A novel? Essays on life and living? They were all possibilities, but none of these ideas went very far nor did they motivate me. I was mostly left with confusion.

So last year I began writing about the process of buying a home and moving to France. It has been fun and creative. With the feedback, it was apparent that a blog would in fact be the easiest thing to do. I could draw on past writing and ideas to build my stories. So far, I have found it stimulating. I hope you do too.

Subscribe if you wish by adding your email at the bottom of the home page. I don’t promise to write according to any particular schedule. I will write when the spirit moves me and I feel I have a story to tell. And I promise I won’t post anything political! I will leave my political activism to Facebook.

Please join me on this adventure
